Terms and conditions

To purchase tickets for Commit, you must accept the following Terms and Conditions:

  • You can only buy tickets by logging into Koliseo using one of the supported authentication providers.
  • When you purchase your ticket, you will be able to enter your billing information during the checkout process and receive the invoice by email along with your ticket. If the invoice is for a company, you will need to provide the company's billing details, including the business name, tax address, and VAT number.
  • In no case will the organization emit a refund unless the event is canceled or rescheduled.
  • If you acquire a Student Ticket, you will have to show your student ID at the event.
  • You can transfer your ticket to someone else by introducing their data on the ticket page or contacting the organization to do the transfer on your behalf.
  • The organization is not responsible for any transferred tickets. It will validate your ticket in the Registration Point, and it will reject it if someone else has presented it.
  • With your ticket, you will receive a badge at the registration desk. You must wear the badge always visible, or you will not be able to attend any talk or lab.

The organization reserves the right to modify these Terms and Conditions at any time and without prior notice.
